The Art of Natural Sweets

Cafe Lavande is a cafe where every macaron, cupcake, and cookie is created with love and dedication to the art of pastry.

We use only the best natural ingredients — organic flour, French butter, Belgian chocolate, and natural flavor extracts . Our goal is to give you an unforgettable taste experience!
